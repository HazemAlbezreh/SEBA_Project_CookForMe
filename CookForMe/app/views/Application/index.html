What we do

CookForMe is a food offering, ordering and delivery platform, to help people passionate about cooking to earn money, and people liking to try various food - to enjoy it.

CookForMe is a new social experience to offer and order food:
1- For people passionate about cooking - opportunity to earn money from something they love.
2- For those keen on trying different cuisine - a variety of home made meals from all over the world.
